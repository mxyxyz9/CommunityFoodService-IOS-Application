# 🍽️✨ SnackShare - Your Community Food Exchange App

Tired of food going to waste? Share it instead! 🙌 Built with **Swift, SwiftUI, SwiftData, and Supabase**, SnackShare makes it easy (and fun) to donate and claim food within your local community.

💡 **Why?** Because sharing is caring, and free food just hits different. 😎

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Home
- **Friendly welcome** (we actually acknowledge you! 🫶)
- **Auto / Manual Location:** Because GPS isn’t always it. 📍
- **Create a post:**
  - Upload a pic 📸
  - Title & deets 📝
  - Veg 🌿 or Non-Veg 🍖?
  - How many ppl can eat? 👥
  - Expiry date? ⏳
- **Food feed:** Sorted by distance, category, and freshness. 🍲

### 🔍 Search
- **Find food fast:** No aimless scrolling. 🚀
- **Filters:**
  - Veg / Non-Veg 🌱🍗
  - Distance 📏
  - How much food? 👥
- **Sort options:** Nearest, freshest, or most popular. 🔄

### 👤 Profile
- **Dark mode toggle:** If you ever wanna switch it up. 🌗
- **Donation history:** Track your food drops. 📜
- **Streak system:** Stay consistent, earn bragging rights. 🔥
- **Leaderboard:** Who’s the MVP of food sharing? 🏅
- **Settings:** Change info, manage notifications, log out. 🚪

## 🚀 User Flows

### 📝 Sign-Up & Login
1. Open the app 📲
2. Pick login method:
   - Phone 📞
   - Google + Phone 🌐📞
   - Apple ID + Phone 🍏📞
3. OTP verification 🔑
4. Set profile, grant location access 📍
5. Boom. You’re in. 🎉

### ✏️ Edit & Delete Posts
1. Tap your post 🏠
2. Hit edit ✏️ (or delete 🗑️)
3. Save changes 💾
4. Done. Easy. ✅

### 🚩 Reporting Sus Stuff
1. Spot something sketchy? 🚩
2. Tap "Report Post" 🛑
3. Select a reason ❗
4. We’ll review it ASAP. 👮‍♂️

### 🔖 Saving Favs
1. Tap the bookmark icon 🔖
2. Find it later in your profile 📑
3. Tap again to remove ❌

### 💬 Chatting w/ Donors
1. Tap a post 🍲
2. Hit "Chat with Donor" 💬
3. Message until post is gone. 🚫
4. Report/block if necessary. 🚷

### 🔔 Staying in the Loop
- Get updates on new food posts 🔔
- Tap notifications to open the post 📱
- Customize alerts in settings ⚙️

### 🏆 Leaderboards & Streaks
- Daily donations = streaks 🔥
- Top donors get featured 🏅
- Earn badges & flex on socials 📢
